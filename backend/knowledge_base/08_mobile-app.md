# Mobile App

> Manage your card and transactions on the go with the Swipey app.

Source: https://help.swipey.co/en/collection/25-mobile-app

---

## Get Started with the Swipey Mobile App

Download:
- [iOS (App Store)](https://apps.apple.com/sa/app/swipey-fin-os-for-modern-biz/id1589412706)
- [Android (Google Play)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=app.swipey.co)

**Setup steps (for invited employees):**
1. Open app → enter email and mobile number
2. Do **not** tap "Register new account"
3. Complete eKYC: upload photo ID + take selfie
4. Name must match MyKad/Passport exactly
5. Once approved, log in to access assigned card

---

## View Transactions

App → **Transactions** tab (top of screen) → view full transaction history.

Employees see only their own transactions. Admins see all (via web dashboard).

---

## Freeze & Reactivate Cards on Mobile

App → **Cards** tab → select card → **Freeze Card**

Card stops working immediately. Balance unaffected.

To restore: **Reactivate Card**

---

## Reset Physical Card PIN

1. App → **Cards** tab → **Reset Card PIN**
2. Authenticate: 6-digit PIN / Face ID / fingerprint
3. Enter OTP sent to registered phone
4. Enter new PIN → Confirm

---

## Pay Merchants via QR

App → **Scan** / QR icon → scan merchant QR code → enter amount → confirm payment.

Supports P2P and B2B QR payments:

| Type | Per Transaction | Daily | Monthly |
|---|---|---|---|
| P2P (personal) | RM 2,000 | RM 2,000 | RM 10,000 |
| B2B (business) | RM 10,000 | RM 50,000 | RM 500,000 |

---

## Reset Mobile App Passcode

If locked out of app:
1. App login screen → **Forgot Passcode**
2. Verify identity via OTP sent to registered phone
3. Set new 6-digit passcode

> If OTP not received, ensure phone number registered matches your Swipey account.
