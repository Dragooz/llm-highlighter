# Funds

> Learn how to top up, load and move money across your accounts.

Source: https://help.swipey.co/en/collection/18-funds

---

## How Long Does It Take for Funds to Be Reflected?

**Master account deposits:**
- **FPX (instant)** — recommended for urgent or weekend deposits
- **Direct bank transfer** — approved at 4 pm Mon–Fri only

**Card top-ups** (if instant top-up is delayed):

| Batch | Cut-off Time | Processing Time |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 10 am | 1–2 hours |
| 2 | 1 pm | 1–2 hours |
| 3 | 5 pm | 1–2 hours |
| 4 | 7 pm | 1–2 hours |

---

## Top-Up Instantly

Admin dashboard → Cards → select card → Top Up → enter amount → confirm. Instant. If delayed, refer to batch schedule above.

Ensure master account has sufficient funds before topping up cards.

---

## Move Funds from Card to Master Account (Clawback)

1. Admin dashboard → Cards → select card
2. Right panel → **Clawback to Master Account**
3. Enter amount → **Retrieve Funds**
4. Enter OTP to confirm

Transfer is instant.

---

## Withdraw Master Account Funds

Admin dashboard → Funds → Withdraw → enter bank details and amount → confirm. Subject to banking processing times.

---

## Auto Top-Up (Growth & Plus Plans Only)

Automatically tops up a card when balance falls below a set threshold.

**Setup:** Cards → select card → right panel → toggle ON Auto Top-Up → set threshold and top-up amount → Save.

Master account must have sufficient balance for auto top-up to work. Cards with auto top-up enabled are marked on the Cards page.

---

## FPX Deposit

Dashboard → Funds → Deposit → select FPX → choose bank → complete payment.

**Fee:** RM 1.50 per transaction (waived on Growth & Plus plans).

**Supported banks:** Major Malaysian banks via FPX network.

---

## Deposit Master Account via Cards

For scenarios where you need to fund via card (e.g., credit card). Dashboard → Funds → Deposit → select card payment option.

---

## Export Master Account Statement

Dashboard → Funds → Export → select date range → download CSV/PDF.

---

## List of Banks Available for FPX Transfers

All major Malaysian banks supported via FPX. Check the full list in dashboard during FPX deposit flow.

## Balance Limits

| | Limit |
|---|---|
| Master account | No maximum |
| Card top-up per card | RM 10,000 |
| Card spending per transaction | RM 10,000 |
| Card spending daily | RM 50,000 |
| Card spending monthly | RM 500,000 |
