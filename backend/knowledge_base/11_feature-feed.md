## 2026

### February 2026 — We Fixed the Most Annoying Part of Finance 🚀

Focus: Efficiency, AI receipts, faster payouts.

**Enhanced Dashboard Filtering**
Filter transactions by: missing receipts, large expenses, unusual activity, items needing reconciliation. Faster month-end close.

**Alfie AI — Receipt Matching**
- Upload a receipt → Alfie auto-matches to correct transaction
- Available on mobile: snap photo on the go, Alfie matches in real-time
- Closes books up to 10× faster

**Fund Master Account via Credit Card**
- Load Swipey Master balance using a credit card
- Funds usable for payroll, supplier invoices, rent, and other payments that previously required cash
- Unlocks high-volume business payments from unused credit lines

**Faster Payouts**
- Bill payments no longer tied to GIRO's fixed disbursement windows
- Same-day processing for submissions before cut-off
- No change to how bills are submitted — just faster delivery

## 2025

### November 2025 — Total Transparency & High-Speed Bills 🌎

Focus: Fee transparency, petrol transaction accuracy, historical data clarity, bug fixes.

**ISA Fee Breakdown in Transaction Details**
Full International Service Assessment fee breakdown now visible per transaction — no manual calculation needed for audit or reconciliation.

**Petrol Station Transaction Corrections**
Transactions where final amount differs from initial authorization (e.g. petrol stations) now auto-update to the final correct amount. Eliminates billing disputes.

**Original Date Referencing for Late Imports**
Late-imported transactions now display the original transaction date, not the import date. Prevents confusion in monthly reconciliations.

**Bug Fixes:**
- Duplicate document no longer created when manually attaching to a bill
- Card request "Sync Data" mobile failures resolved
- Fund transfer transaction statuses and amounts now correctly displayed
