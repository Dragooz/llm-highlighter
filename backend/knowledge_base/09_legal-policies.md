## Feedback & Complaints Policy

Swipey is committed to resolving issues fairly and promptly.

### How to Lodge a Complaint

| Channel | Details |
|---|---|
| WhatsApp | [+603 9213 1099](https://api.whatsapp.com/send?phone=60392131099) — fastest response |
| Email | hi@swipey.co — include company name and issue description |
| Help Center | help.swipey.co |

### What to Include

- Name and registered company name
- Clear description of the issue (what, when, how)
- Relevant transaction IDs, dates, screenshots
- Desired outcome / resolution sought

### Resolution Timeline

| Stage | Timeframe | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Acknowledgement | Within 4 business hours | Complaint received, assigned to team |
| Investigation | 7–14 business days | Case reviewed, info gathered |
| Resolution | Up to 14 business days | Final response + remedial steps if applicable |

> Cases involving third parties (card networks, banking partners) may take longer — outside Swipey's direct control.

## T&C: Bill and Invoice Pay-out Product Campaign ("Lowest FX Rate Guaranteed")

**What it means:** Swipey guarantees the lowest effective FX rate — covering the FX rate itself plus all cross-border fees — compared to B2B/B2C remittance providers (excludes consumer-focused platforms like Wise).

**To claim a lower rate:**
1. Submit via [claim form](https://forms.clickup.com/3820803/f/3mk83-45996/8SS07IPN0P3YDPIK91)
2. Provide proof: same country, same currency, same date
3. If Swipey confirms a better rate existed — difference credited to company account

Swipey reserves the right to update these T&Cs at any time.

## T&C: Cashback Program

**Program:** 1% cashback on eligible digital marketing and SaaS subscription spend, capped at **RM 75 per company per month**.

**Eligibility:**
- All verified Swipey users
- Participants must not have transacted via Swipey since 1 January 2025

**Duration:** 1 March 2026 – 31 May 2026

**Redemption:**
- Cashback credited to Company Master Account within 20 days after month-end
- No expiry on cashback
- No restriction on use — spend at any merchant/vendor via Swipey
- Cannot be combined with previously earned cashback; cannot be withdrawn as cash

Swipey reserves the right to modify, cancel, or suspend the program at any time.
