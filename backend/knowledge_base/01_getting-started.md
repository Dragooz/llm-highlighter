# Getting Started

> Step-by-step guides to set up Swipey in the right order.

Source: https://help.swipey.co/en/collection/16-getting-started

---

## Admin Guide

### 1. Introduction to Swipey
Swipey is a business finance platform offering prepaid VISA cards, payouts/bill payment, and accounting integration for teams.

### 2. Sign Up to Swipey
Register your company at [app.swipey.co](https://app.swipey.co). Complete company verification during onboarding.

### 3. Fund Your Master Account
Deposit funds into your Swipey master account via:
- **FPX (instant)** — recommended; fees waived on Growth & Plus plans
- **Direct bank transfer** — approved at 4 pm Mon–Fri

Master account has no maximum limit.

### 4. Invite Your Team
1. Log in to admin dashboard → Profile (bottom left) → **Employees** tab → **Invite Employee**
2. Fill in name, email, phone, and assign a role (Employee / Admin / Bookkeeper)
3. Card assignment is optional at this stage — can be done later
4. Employee receives email invite → downloads Swipey app → completes eKYC

> Each employee must have a unique email and phone number not previously used on Swipey.

### 5. Assign Cards to Your Team
Two card types available:

| | Virtual Card | Physical Card |
|---|---|---|
| Availability | All plans | Growth & Plus only |
| Delivery | Instant | 7–10 business days |
| Use | Online only | Online + in-store |

**Steps:**
1. Admin dashboard → **Cards**
2. Select employee → **Issue a New Card**
3. Set card type, spending limit, card name, and optional merchant restrictions
4. Submit

> Start with virtual cards for faster setup.

### 6. Top Up Cards
From admin dashboard → Cards → select card → **Top Up**. Instant top-up available. If delayed, batches process at 10am / 1pm / 5pm / 7pm.

### 7. Attach Receipts
Employees attach receipts per transaction via mobile app → Transactions tab → select transaction → attach photo.

### 8. Make Your First Transaction
Use card details (virtual) or physical card at any Visa-accepted merchant.

## Employee Guide

### 1. Get Started with Mobile App
1. Receive email invite from admin
2. Download Swipey app: [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/sa/app/swipey-fin-os-for-modern-biz/id1589412706) | [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=app.swipey.co)
3. Enter email + mobile number (do NOT click "Register new account")
4. Complete eKYC (photo ID + selfie; name must match MyKad/Passport exactly)

### 2. Virtual vs Physical Cards
- **Virtual**: Available in app immediately after setup. Use for online payments only.
- **Physical**: Delivered by post in 7–10 business days. Use anywhere Visa accepted.
- For first physical card in-store payment: always use **Chip and PIN** to activate PayWave.

### 3. Making Payments with a Swipey Card
- **Online**: Use card number, expiry, CVV from app (Cards tab → Show Card Details)
- **In-store**: Insert physical card + PIN, or tap (PayWave) after PIN activation
- **QR**: Pay merchants via QR from mobile app
