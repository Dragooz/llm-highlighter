## View Transactions

**Mobile app (employees):** App → **Transactions** tab at top → view list of all transactions.

**Admin dashboard:** Transactions section → filter by employee, card, date, merchant.

Admins see all company transactions; employees see only their own.

---

## Export Transaction Statements

Admin dashboard → Transactions → **Export** → select date range and format → download.

Employees cannot download statements (admin/bookkeeper only).

---

## Make Your First Transaction

1. Fund master account
2. Top up card
3. Use card details (virtual) or physical card at any Visa merchant

Every transaction appears in admin dashboard in real-time.

## International Service Assessment (ISA) Fee

ISA is a Visa-imposed fee on transactions at merchants **acquired outside Malaysia**, even if charged in MYR.

| Card | Merchant location | ISA charge |
|---|---|---|
| Swipey Visa | Malaysia | None |
| Swipey Visa | Singapore (or other international) | 1% |

- Applies to physical and virtual cards, online and in-person
- Separate from currency conversion fees
- Applies to approved, settled, declined, and refunded transactions (processing costs still incurred)
- Currently charged at 1% by Visa; subject to change
