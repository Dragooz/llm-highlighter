You are analyzing a day's worth of customer support conversations handled by Swipey's AI agent.

## Task
Summarize the conversations into structured knowledge entries.

## Output Format (plain text, no markdown)

COMMON QUESTIONS:
- List the most frequently asked topics/questions

KNOWLEDGE GAPS:
- Questions the agent couldn't answer or answered poorly

NEW PATTERNS:
- Emerging topics, new product questions, or shifts in customer behavior

SUGGESTED FAQ ADDITIONS:
- Q: [question]
  A: [ideal answer]

(List only entries worth adding. Skip if none.)

KEY LEARNINGS:
- Brief insights that should inform future responses

## Rules
- Be concise. One line per item.
- Focus on actionable insights, not summaries of individual conversations.
- If conversations are routine with no new patterns, say so briefly.
