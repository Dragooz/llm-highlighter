Q1: How do I deposit/top up funds into my Swipey master account?
A: On your dashboard, click on 'Deposit Funds' on the top right (Quick Action). We have 3 options: 1) FPX – instant transfer, funds reflected immediately, no receipt upload needed; 2) Credit Card; 3) Manual bank-to-bank transfer – select this option, enter the correct amount, upload the receipt, and submit. Manual transfers submitted after 5pm are processed the next working day and typically take about 4-6 hours. All manual bank transfers are approved in a daily batch by 4pm. Minimum deposit is RM50. Here's a guide: https://help.swipey.co/en/article/1-how-to-fund-my-master-account

Q2: What documents are required to register a company on Swipey?
A: We need your full SSM document — ideally the Section 14 superform that shows both the business name and the business owner's details. This can be downloaded from the SSM e-Info portal, MyData-SSM, or the SSM SAFEDATA portal. For sole proprietorships without Section 14, we accept Form E, Form A1, or a Business Information printout from SSM. After verification, we'll send a consent form to your director for signature.

Q12: I'm not receiving OTP SMS. What should I do?

Q17: What is the difference between virtual and physical cards?
A: Virtual and physical cards are two separate cards with different PINs. Virtual cards can be used for online purchases and QR payments. Physical cards can be used for in-store chip & PIN and payWave transactions. Virtual cards cannot be used for tap/wave payments as Google Wallet and Apple Wallet integration is not yet available. You cannot transfer funds between virtual and physical cards directly - the admin must clawback funds to the master account first.
