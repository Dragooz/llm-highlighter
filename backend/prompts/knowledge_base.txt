# Getting Started

> Step-by-step guides to set up Swipey in the right order.

Source: https://help.swipey.co/en/collection/16-getting-started

---

## Admin Guide

### 1. Introduction to Swipey
Swipey is a business finance platform offering prepaid VISA cards, payouts/bill payment, and accounting integration for teams.

### 2. Sign Up to Swipey
Register your company at [app.swipey.co](https://app.swipey.co). Complete company verification during onboarding.

### 3. Fund Your Master Account
Deposit funds into your Swipey master account via:
- **FPX (instant)** — recommended; fees waived on Growth & Plus plans
- **Direct bank transfer** — approved at 4 pm Mon–Fri

Master account has no maximum limit.

### 4. Invite Your Team
1. Log in to admin dashboard → Profile (bottom left) → **Employees** tab → **Invite Employee**
2. Fill in name, email, phone, and assign a role (Employee / Admin / Bookkeeper)
3. Card assignment is optional at this stage — can be done later
4. Employee receives email invite → downloads Swipey app → completes eKYC

> Each employee must have a unique email and phone number not previously used on Swipey.

### 5. Assign Cards to Your Team
Two card types available:

| | Virtual Card | Physical Card |
|---|---|---|
| Availability | All plans | Growth & Plus only |
| Delivery | Instant | 7–10 business days |
| Use | Online only | Online + in-store |

**Steps:**
1. Admin dashboard → **Cards**
2. Select employee → **Issue a New Card**
3. Set card type, spending limit, card name, and optional merchant restrictions
4. Submit

> Start with virtual cards for faster setup.

### 6. Top Up Cards
From admin dashboard → Cards → select card → **Top Up**. Instant top-up available. If delayed, batches process at 10am / 1pm / 5pm / 7pm.

### 7. Attach Receipts
Employees attach receipts per transaction via mobile app → Transactions tab → select transaction → attach photo.

### 8. Make Your First Transaction
Use card details (virtual) or physical card at any Visa-accepted merchant.

## Employee Guide

### 1. Get Started with Mobile App
1. Receive email invite from admin
2. Download Swipey app: [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/sa/app/swipey-fin-os-for-modern-biz/id1589412706) | [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=app.swipey.co)
3. Enter email + mobile number (do NOT click "Register new account")
4. Complete eKYC (photo ID + selfie; name must match MyKad/Passport exactly)

### 2. Virtual vs Physical Cards
- **Virtual**: Available in app immediately after setup. Use for online payments only.
- **Physical**: Delivered by post in 7–10 business days. Use anywhere Visa accepted.
- For first physical card in-store payment: always use **Chip and PIN** to activate PayWave.

### 3. Making Payments with a Swipey Card
- **Online**: Use card number, expiry, CVV from app (Cards tab → Show Card Details)
- **In-store**: Insert physical card + PIN, or tap (PayWave) after PIN activation
- **QR**: Pay merchants via QR from mobile app

## Freeze & Reactivate Cards

Freezing blocks all new transactions instantly. Balance unaffected.

**Via mobile app (employee or admin):**
Cards tab → select card → **Freeze Card** / **Reactivate Card**

**Via admin dashboard (admin only):**
Cards → select card → **Freeze Card** / **Reactivate Card**

> Frozen card must be reactivated before it can be terminated.
> Freezing is temporary; terminating is permanent.

## Payouts Introduction

Payouts helps businesses manage, review, schedule and pay vendor invoices and bills. Supports **local bank transfer, JomPay, and foreign disbursement**.

Number of bills per plan varies — see [swipey.co/pricing](https://swipey.co/pricing/).

---

### 1. Bill Capture

Forward PDF/Excel/Word invoices to **bills@swipey.co** → auto-captured and shown in Kanban view.

**Rules:**
- Only registered Swipey users can forward bills (non-registered emails ignored)
- Max 10 invoices per email
- Only invoices/bills — EPF, salary slips, payroll reports not processed
- Processing: ~5 mins, up to 2 hours
- Bills show "Processing" label while being extracted

---

### 2. Managing Bills — Kanban Stages

| Status | Description |
|---|---|
| **Pending Review** | New bills land here. Review vendor, amount, currency, bank details. AI auto-extracts but user must verify accuracy. |
| **To Approve** | Bill reviewed and ready for approval. Must set "Pay On" date before moving to Scheduled. |
| **Scheduled** | Ready for payment. Master account deducted immediately to ring-fence funds. Can be moved out before pay date to release funds. |
| **Paid** | Disbursed. Stays here 30 days; older paid bills in Funds tab. |
| **Rejected** | Rejected by user or payment failed (wrong bank details). |

**Review checklist per bill:**
1. Vendor name
2. Charged amount
3. Currency
4. Bank details

---

### 3. Vendor Management

- First-time vendor: add details from inner bill page OR Settings → Vendor → Add New Vendor
- Repeat vendor: bank details auto-populated from Vendor Master List
- Vendor details saved to master list for future reuse

**Supported foreign currencies:**
AUD, USD, EUR, GBP, SGD, CNY, PHP, IDR, PKR, BDT + 100+ countries for disbursement.

> Reach out for currencies not listed above.

---

### 4. Scheduling & Making Payment

1. Open bill → set **Pay on Date** (today or future)
2. Move to **Scheduled** (click "Approve" or select Scheduled from dropdown)
3. Bill auto-paid on scheduled date

**Payment cut-off times (GIRO network):**

| Swipey Cut-off | Disbursed | Cleared by |
|---|---|---|
| 10:00 am | 12:30 pm | 12:30 pm same day |
| 1:00 pm | 4:30 pm | 4:30 pm same day |
| 5:00 pm | 7:00 pm | 7:00 pm same day |
| After 5:00 pm | 10:00 am next day | 10:00 am next day |

> No processing on weekends or Malaysian public holidays.
> Cross-border payments: 1–3 working days.

## QuickBooks Integration

### Step 1: Set Up QuickBooks Cash Account

Before linking, create a cash account in QuickBooks to represent Swipey funds:

1. Log in to QuickBooks
2. Chart of Accounts → **New** → Account Type: Cash and Cash Equivalents
3. Name it (e.g., "Swipey Master Account")
4. Repeat for Swipey Cards Account

### Step 2: Link QuickBooks to Swipey

1. Swipey dashboard → **Accounting** → **Connect to QuickBooks**
2. Click **Connect**
3. Authenticate with QuickBooks credentials
4. Map **Swipey Master Account** → your QuickBooks ledger account
5. Map **Swipey Cards Account** → your QuickBooks ledger account
6. Confirm — all linked accounts show green "Linked" label

> **Important:** Do not disconnect QuickBooks after linking — disconnecting may cause unsaved changes. Swipey is working on improving this.

### Step 3: Sync Transactions to QuickBooks

Swipey dashboard → Accounting → **Sync** → transactions pushed to mapped QuickBooks accounts.

- **Expenses** = card transactions + bill payments
- **Internal Transfers** = master account movements (deposit, top-up, clawback)

---

## Xero Integration

### Step 1: Create Cash Accounts in Xero

1. Log in to Xero → **Accounting** → **Chart of Accounts**
2. **Add Bank Account** → **Add without Bank Feed**
3. Name it (e.g., "Swipey Master Account")
4. Repeat for Swipey Cards Account

### Step 2: Connect Xero to Swipey

1. Swipey dashboard → **Accounting Integration** → **Connect to Xero**
2. Authenticate with Xero credentials

### Step 3: Map Accounts

After connecting:
- Select **Swipey Master Account** → map to Xero cash account created in Step 1
- Select **Swipey Cards Account** → map to Xero cards account

### Step 4: Sync Transactions

Accounting → click **Expense** or **Internal Transfers** to start syncing:

| Sync Type | What It Covers |
|---|---|
| Expense | Card transactions & bill payments |
| Internal Transfers | Deposit, top-up, clawback |

---

## Syncing Transactions to QuickBooks / Xero

- Transactions sync automatically or on-demand
- Receipts and notes attached in Swipey carry over
- Match synced transactions in QuickBooks/Xero for reconciliation

> Keep accounting software connected at all times for uninterrupted sync.

## Suspicious Transactions

### Prevention
- Never share card details or CVV
- Never share OTPs used to approve transactions
- Never pass card to others to use

### Common Causes of Unrecognized Charges (Not Fraud)
- Petrol station pre-authorizations
- Offline transactions
- Unfamiliar business names (merchant trade names vs brand names)
- Small verification charges
- Auto-renewed subscription services

### If You Suspect Fraud
1. **Freeze card immediately** via Swipey mobile app
2. Rule out the common causes above
3. Contact Swipey Customer Success to terminate and replace card

### How to Report
Email **help@swipey.co** with:
- Email linked to Swipey account
- Last 4 digits of affected card
- Transaction ID
- Transaction details (date, time, merchant, amount)

Swipey prepares a Visa dispute request form for your confirmation.

### Dispute Process & Timelines
- Investigation by Visa: **up to 180 days** from submission
- Dispute **does not guarantee refund** — subject to Visa's final decision
- **Minimum dispute amount:** USD 25 per transaction (or local currency equivalent)
- Under Visa Dispute Condition 10.4: max **35 transactions** per submission for unauthorized/fraudulent category; **no limit** for other dispute categories (e.g. service not received, duplicate charge)
